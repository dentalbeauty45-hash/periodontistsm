Even when treated by an experienced Dental Implants Periodontist, wound infections can sometimes occur after dental implant surgery. A skilled Dental Implants Periodontist takes every precaution to minimize the risk of infection, including using sterile techniques, providing detailed post-operative care instructions, and monitoring healing closely.
